<h1 class="my-3 text-[2rem]">Privacy Policy</h1>

<p class="text-lg my-4">Startify does not store any information about or from you, besides error reports. Your settings are saved securely within your browser and never transmitted to us.</p>

<h1 class="text-[20px] font-bold">Scope</h1>

<p class="text-lg my-4">This privacy policy covers the Startify browser extension.</p>

<h1 class="text-[20px] font-bold">Terms</h1>

<div class="text-lg my-4 flex flex-col">
<p><span class="font-bold">- Startify</span> - The Startify browser extension and web version</p>
<p><span class="font-bold">- We/Us</span> - The developer of Startify</p></div>

<h1 class="text-[20px] font-bold">Settings</h1>

<p class="text-lg my-4">Your settings control what Startify displays on your page. Your settings are stored securely within your browser. We do not store a copy of your settings.</p>

<h1 class="text-[20px] font-bold">Error Reports</h1>

<p class="text-lg my-4">
Startify does not automatically collect error data. If you encounter a bug, please report it through our GitHub repository.
</p>

<h1 class="text-[20px] font-bold">No Tracking</h1>

<p class="text-lg my-4">
Startify does not track your activity or collect personal information while you use the extension.</p>

<h1 class="text-[20px] font-bold">Policy Updates</h1>

<p class="text-lg my-4">
We will occasionally update this privacy policy and publish the changes here. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our github</p>

<h1 class="text-[20px] font-bold">Contact Us</h1>

<p class="text-lg my-4">For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, you can reach us via our GitHub repository.</p>
